# ABOUT WEBSITE

Website called LETAK, which will serve as my personal art gallery and virtual shop. This website will be a platform where visitors can explore my artwork.

The website will not list prices, as the primary focus right now is to showcase my works and story behind work. Visitors will have the opportunity to contact me to inquire whether a particular piece is available for sale. The site will include sections providing information about my artistic vision, my biography, contact details, as well as links to my social media profiles.

The project is being developed as a personal platform to showcase my work and allow those interested in my art to easily reach out to me.
